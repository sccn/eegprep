POP_CLEAN_RAWDATA - Launch the clean_rawdata artifact-cleaning workflow.

Usage:
  EEG = pop_clean_rawdata(EEG, 'key', value, ...)

Calling pop_clean_rawdata(EEG) opens the EEGPrep GUI dialog for configuring
high-pass drift removal, channel cleaning, ASR burst correction or rejection,
and additional bad-window removal.
